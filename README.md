## Overview

This repository contains the research compendium and [Quarto
book](https://quarto.org/docs/books/) of my master’s thesis: _Associations between the duration and quality of sleep of pregnant women in the third trimester with the duration of labor_. Its aim is to facilitate research work and to improve reproducibility.
